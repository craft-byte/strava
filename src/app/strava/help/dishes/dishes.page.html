Adding dishes to your menu

To add your dishes to your menu you need to first click on your created restaurant on the account dashboard, it'll bring you to the restaurant dashboard. Then, on the restaurant dashboard click on dishes tab. You'll see add your dish button, click it.

Now on adding dish page you can add dish with image, time cooking, description, category. Required fields are name and price, everything else is up to you.

Tip: The more information you provide about your dish the prettier it'll look on your menu.

Click save and you'll see your dish on the restaurant dashboard.
