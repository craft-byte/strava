Get Started With Strava

Welcome. You're about to create your restaurant on Strava. For that your restaurant has to be in Canada. You'll have to provide your name, date of birth, bank account, and restaurant address.

To start you have to first create an account on the register page. Then to create your restaurant you will have to verify your account by entering code that was sent to your email address.

When your account has been verified you can get to creating your restaurant. On your account dashboard in add restaurant box click on start button. Follow the instructions and fill the fields required.

Your name, date of birth, and bank account are required to verify that you are real. They will not be shared.

Your restaurant should have been created now. If you can't see it on your account dashboard, please reload the page. If you still don't see the restaurant contact us.
